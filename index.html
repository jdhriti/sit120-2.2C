ARCTIC MONKEYS

Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002. The band includes Alex Turner (lead vocals, guitar, keyboards), Jamie Cook (guitar, keyboards), Nick O'Malley (bass guitar, backing vocals), and Matt Helders (drums). Andy Nicholson (bass guitar, backing vocals) quit the band in 2006 following their debut album.
